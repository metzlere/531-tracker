5/3/1 Program Schedule

Week Set 1 Set 2 Set 3
Week 1 65% - 5 75% - 5 85% - 5+
Week 2 70% - 3 80% - 3 90% - 3+
Week 3 75% - 5 85% - 3 95% - 1+
Deload Week 40% - 5 50% - 5 60% - 5

Note: All percentages are calculated using 90% of your true 1RM (your training max). The prescribed sets and reps above are for Squat, Bench, Deadlift, and Press. Add accessory work as desired based on your specific goals.

The 5/3/1 program, developed by Jim Wendler, is designed to help build strength steadily while ensuring long-term progress.
